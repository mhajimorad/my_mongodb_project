So that the forum is a welcoming place for all, here are some ground rules:

  • Consider the different dimensions to comments you post. Would someone with a different lived experience and/or identity than you see the comment in the same way? Positive, encouraging messages are greatly appreciated and encouraged!

  • Post messages in the appropriate channels to keep things organized. Feel free to suggest new channels, which can be added by the administrator


By creating an account and posting messages to the forum, you are agreeing with the aforementioned ground rules
